Community Guidelines

Be respectful to all those with whom you interact within our community.

Share comments and ideas thoughtfully, with the consideration you would expect to be afforded.

Refrain from inappropriate, off-topic, abusive, harassing, or profane comments.

Do not post information that would be considered spam.

We reserve the right to reject or remove comments that are not in keeping with our policy, values, and principles; and to block or ban users/followers who do not follow these guidelines.

Original posts are to be auto-deleted one week after the requested date on the form.
